Real Life Uses

U

Nuclear Fuel:

Uranium is primarily used as fuel in nuclear reactors due to its ability to sustain fission reactions.
Used in: Nuclear power generation, military submarines, nuclear weapons.

Th

Emerging Reactor Fuel:

Thorium is being explored as a safer and more abundant alternative to uranium in next-gen nuclear reactors.
Used in: Experimental thorium reactors, high-temperature ceramics, and gas mantles.

Pu

Spacecraft Power:

Plutonium-238 is used in RTGs to provide long-term power in space missions.
Used in: NASA missions like Voyager, Curiosity rover, and deep-space probes.

Am

Smoke Detectors:

Americium-241 emits alpha particles used in ionization-type smoke detectors.
Used in: Home safety devices, industrial gauges, and density measurement tools.

Cf

Neutron Source:

Californium-252 is a strong neutron emitter, used in scientific and industrial neutron radiography.
Used in: Oil exploration, neutron activation analysis, cancer treatment trials.
